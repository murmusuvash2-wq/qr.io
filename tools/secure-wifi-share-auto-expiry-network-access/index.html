FAQ
Q: Can guests connect without typing the password?
A: Yes, scanning the standard WiFi QR protocol prompts a direct 'Join Network' button on Android and iOS, allowing users to connect with a single tap.
Q: Does the QR reveal my password?
A: The credentials (SSID and password) are encoded directly inside the pattern. While camera apps read the security parameters automatically to connect, the raw password is not visible in plain text on the printed design itself.
Q: Will it work on iPhone?
A: Yes! iPhones running iOS 11 and above have native, built-in WiFi QR recognition. Simply aim the camera, tap the 'Join' notification banner, and connect.
Q: Will it work on Android?
A: Yes. Android devices have natively integrated support for the standard WIFI:S: protocol. It works flawlessly across all Android models.
Q: Can I print the QR near my router?
A: Yes! Printing and sticking a clean WiFi QR code on your router or living room wall is highly recommended for smart homes, Airbnb hosts, and office workspaces.
